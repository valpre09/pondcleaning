Pondovac 4 Specifications

The Pondovac 4 is a professional pond vacuum designed for efficient cleaning of medium to large ponds with the following capabilities:

Maximum Depth

2 meters (6.5 feet)

Tank Capacity

30 liters (8 gallons)

Suction Power

1600W motor for powerful suction

Water Usage

Minimal water loss during cleaning

Note: Our Pondovac 4 is ideal for ponds up to 2 meters deep. For deeper ponds, please contact us for alternative solutions.
